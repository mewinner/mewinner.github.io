Vodka Pasta

Taken from @stephpappas TikTok

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of vodka
  • A pinch of salt and pepper
  • 2 cups of brown rice pasta from Trader Joe's
  • 15 ounce can of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream

Instructions:

  1. Chop up your onion.
  2. Mince the cloves of garlic.
  3. Put in butter and olive oil into a pan.
  4. Put the in the onion, garlic, and salt and pepper into the pan and let simmer on low heat.
  5. Add in the vodka and let it cook for 15 minutes
  6. While the vodka is cooking, start boiling water for the pasta
  7. Cook the pasta
  8. Add tomato paste and heavy whipping cream to sauce
  9. Let sauce simmer on low heat
  10. Add pasta to your sauce and enjoy!

Salmon Rice Bowl

Taken from @emilymariko TikTok

Ingredients:

  • 1 filet of cooked salmon
  • 1 pack of jasmine rice from Trader Joe's
  • Soy Sauce
  • Siracha
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Avocado
  • Pack of Dry Seaweed from Trader Joe's

Instructions:

  1. Cook 1 filet of salmon to your liking
  2. Mash up the salmon
  3. Cook the rice for 3 minutes and add to the salmon picture
  4. Add as much Gluten Free Soy Sauce, Siracha, and Kewpie mayo to your liking
  5. Mix up the mixture of the Salmon, Rice and Sauces
  6. Add the avocado and any other vegetables you want on top
  7. Add the seaweed on the side
  8. Enjoy your creation!
